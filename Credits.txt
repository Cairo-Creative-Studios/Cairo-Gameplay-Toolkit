While most of the Toolit was written by Richy Mackro from Ciaro Creative Studios, there are many features that wouldn't have been possible without the contributions of other open source projects on Github,
here you can find a list of open source projects that have been used in the production of the Toolkit:

- SaveGameFree, by Bayat Games - https://github.com/BayatGames/SaveGameFree
For saving and Loading

- Randomation-Vehicle-Physics, by JustInvoke - https://github.com/JustInvoke/Randomation-Vehicle-Physics
For Physics of the Car Driver

- SelectionHistory, by Peaj - https://github.com/Peaj/Unitility.git?path=/Packages/de.peaj.selectionhistory
For Selection History

- kSplines, by Kink3d - https://openupm.com/packages/com.kink3d.splines/
For creation and use of Splines

- editor-tools, by faster-games - https://github.com/faster-games/editor-tools/releases
For Searchable Pop Ups in the Editor

- Hierarchy Decorator, by WooshiiDev - https://github.com/WooshiiDev/HierarchyDecorator/releases
For making a more functional Hierarchy

- Tween Components, by Kitbashery - https://github.com/Kitbashery/Tween-Components
For Tweening with the Tween Driver

- Nudge, by stevehalliwell - https://github.com/SAM-tak/Nudge
For adding comments attached to Scene Objects or Project Assets

- Modular-AI, by Kitbashery - https://github.com/Kitbashery/Modular-AI
For basic AI in the AI Agent Behaviour

- Asset Palette, by Roy Theunissen - https://github.com/RoyTheunissen/Asset-Palette
For simple Level Designing with the Asset Palette

- Scene View Picker, by Roy Theunissen - https://github.com/RoyTheunissen/Scene-View-Picker
For Object Picking in the Editor

- Naughty Attributes, by dbrizov - https://github.com/dbrizov/NaughtyAttributes
For it's many glorious Inspector Attributes

- Game Development Extensions, by dotBunny - https://github.com/dotBunny/GDX
For it's amazing collection of useful extension methods!

- Inspector Foldout Group, by PixeyeHQ - https://github.com/PixeyeHQ/InspectorFoldoutGroup
Helping in keeping the Toolkit organzed since it's inclusion
